Contacts

We offer weighting cups produced by our original technology.

Weighting cups are used for laboratory testing, examination and storage of samples of different materials such as soils, sand mixtures, etc. Also, they can be used as penetration cups and vessels for measuring the moisture content of materials in a drying oven and other laboratory equipment.

Areas of application include the building sector, highway engineering, geological surveying, photography, chemical, petroleum refining, paint and varnish industries. The most used size of a weighing cup is 50x37 mm.

We manufacture a wide range of articles from sheet metal (aluminum, titanium, brass and different steel grades). Our original technology features a number of peculiarities that make it make it more versatile and efficient in comparison with common metal forming operations. Our customers are attracted by high quality of our products and competitive prices.

Fist, we developed the technology for producing aluminum weighing cups with size 50x37 mm (also known as BC-1) and then for larger sizes. At present we produce more that 20 dimension-types of weighing cups made from aluminum alloys.

The advantages of our technology include:

1) versatility and quick readjustment of equipment, so that a new model can be launched into mass production within 1-2 months
2) possibility for producing small batches of articles (beginning from 100 pieces) while for the common sheet forming technology the minimal batch is 5000 pieces.
3) after the metal forming operation all the articles are subjected to calibration, so the lid does not seize as is common in cups produced by traditional technologies
4) relatively low prices.
